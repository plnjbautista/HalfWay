About HalfWay

HalfWay is an interactive web based tool designed to visualize and demonstrate the Bisection Method, a fundamental numerical technique used to find the roots of nonlinear equations.

This project was developed in partial fulfillment of the subject CCS 239 Optimization Theory and Applications and was created by a fourth year Bachelor of Science in Computer Science student from section 4B AI.
